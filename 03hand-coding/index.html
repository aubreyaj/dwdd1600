Why learn about fashion history?

Clothing is so much more than something to wear or look at. It has been a part of human life for centuries and plays meaningful roles in many aspects of life and society; in the past and today.

Some significant roles of clothing are:

  • Practical Function and Protection
  • Religious or Spiritual Symbolism
  • Cultural Rituals
  • Status Representation
  • Career Status
  • Self Expression

Clothing has so many uses and meanings. From simple everyday use to big cultural statements, clothing plays important parts in peopleâ€™s lives, whether they think about it or not.

What are some significant parts of history fashion played a part in during the last century?

  1. The Industrial Revolution
  2. Changes In Women's Roles
  3. World War 1 and World War 2
  4. Cultural Movemets and Statements
  5. The Evolution of Consumerism
  6. Evolution of Media

These significant events and fashion have interacted in a way that creates the styles and clothing that we are used to seeing and wearing today, from technological innovations to changes in cultural expectation.
Try visiting a fashion museum to see how history and clothing have evolved together. The FIT museum in New York has an online gallery as well as a physical gallery featuring clothing from the last couple of centuries.
